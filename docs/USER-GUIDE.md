## 2. For Students

### Logging In

1. Go to **attaqwa.org/student/login**
2. Choose your login method:
   - **Email** -- Enter your registered email and password
   - **Student ID** -- Enter your student ID number and password
3. Optionally check **"Remember me"** to stay logged in
4. Click **"Login to Student Portal"**

> **Forgot your password?** Click the "Forgot password?" link on the login page to reset it via email.

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard (`/student/dashboard`) shows everything at a glance:

- **Courses Completed** -- How many courses you've finished
- **GPA** -- Your current grade point average (out of 4.00)
- **Active Classes** -- Number of classes you're currently enrolled in
- **GPA Comparison Chart** -- See how your GPA compares across semesters
- **Daily Class Schedule** -- Today's classes with times and locations
- **Payment & Tuition History** -- Recent financial transactions

### Academic Features

#### My Courses (`/student/courses`)
View all your enrolled courses with:
- Course title and instructor name
- Progress bar showing completion percentage
- Number of lessons in each course
- Click any course to access its lessons

#### My Lessons (`/student/lessons`)
Browse all 25+ available lessons:
- Search lessons by name
- Filter by type (video, reading, interactive, quiz, practice)
- See duration and difficulty for each lesson
- Click **"Start"** to begin a lesson

#### Browse Courses (`/student/browse`)
Discover new courses to enroll in:
- Filter by category (Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, Seerah, Arabic, and more)
- See course difficulty (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- View instructor information and course descriptions

#### Assignments (`/student/assignments`)
Track your homework and projects:
- **Pending** -- Assignments waiting to be completed
- **Submitted** -- Work you've turned in
- **Graded** -- Assignments with scores and feedback
- Each assignment shows point values and due dates

#### Grades & Transcript (`/student/grades`, `/student/transcript`)
- View your cumulative GPA and semester-by-semester breakdown
- See individual course scores
- Download your **official transcript as PDF**
- View grading scale and program information

#### Class Schedule (`/student/schedule`)
- Weekly calendar grid view (Monday through Sunday)
- Color-coded by subject for easy reading
- Shows class times, locations, and instructors

#### Islamic Calendar (`/student/calendar`)
- Dual Gregorian and Hijri date display
- Important Islamic dates and events highlighted
- Academic deadlines and school events

### Documents

| Document | What You Get |
|----------|-------------|
| Official Transcript | Your complete academic record with PDF download |
| Certificates | Certificates for completed courses, each with a unique credential ID |

> **Tip:** Download certificates to share on social media or include in applications.

### Financial Information

| Page | Features |

| Tuition (`/student/tuition`) | Itemized fee breakdown, payment progress bar, auto-pay setup |

### Student Life

## 6. Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

**For Students:**
1. Visit the masjid office or contact us online at **attaqwa.org/contact**
2. Provide your name, email address, and phone number
3. You will receive a welcome email with your login credentials
4. Log in at **attaqwa.org/student/login** to begin

**For Teachers:**
1. Contact the masjid administration
2. Your teacher account will be created with appropriate permissions
3. You will receive login credentials via email
4. Log in at **attaqwa.org/teacher/login**

**For Administrators:**
1. Admin accounts are created by the Super Administrator
2. Log in at **admin.attaqwa.org**

### Your First Login

1. Open your login page (student, teacher, or admin)
2. Enter the email and password from your welcome email
3. **Change your password** on first login for security
4. Explore your dashboard to familiarize yourself with the layout
5. Update your profile with your name and photo

> **Tip:** If you're having trouble logging in, make sure you're using the correct portal. Students log in at `/student/login`, teachers at `/teacher/login`, and admins at the separate admin site.

## 7. Accessibility & Language Support

### Arabic & RTL Support

The platform fully supports Arabic text with right-to-left (RTL) formatting:
- Quranic verses display in Uthmani script using the **Amiri** font
- Arabic text automatically renders in the correct direction
- Bilingual content appears in both English and Arabic where appropriate

### Mobile-Friendly Design

The entire platform is responsive and works on:
- Desktop computers and laptops
- Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
- Mobile phones (iPhone, Android)

All pages automatically adjust to your screen size. The navigation collapses into a mobile-friendly menu on smaller screens.

### Design & Colors

Our platform uses an Islamic-inspired color palette:
- **Islamic Green** -- Primary color for actions and navigation
- **Islamic Gold** -- Highlights and important information
- **Islamic Navy** -- Text and formal elements

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need an account to view prayer times?**
A: No. Prayer times, services, resources, and all public pages are available without logging in.

**Q: What devices can I use?**
A: Any device with a web browser -- computer, tablet, or phone. The site adapts to your screen size.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes. We use encrypted connections, secure authentication, and follow data privacy best practices. See our Privacy Policy at `/privacy`.

### Students

**Q: How do I enroll in a course?**
A: Visit **Browse Courses** (`/student/browse`), find a course you're interested in, and click to enroll. Some courses may require admin approval.

**Q: How do I download my transcript?**
A: Go to **Transcript** (`/student/transcript`) and click the **PDF download** button.

**Q: Can I join a study group?**
A: Yes! Visit **Study Groups** (`/student/groups`) to browse existing groups or create your own.

**Q: How do I apply for financial aid?**
A: Visit **Financial Aid** (`/student/financial-aid`) to check eligibility and submit an application for Zakat Aid.

### Teachers

**Q: How do I create a new lesson?**
A: Go to **Lesson Content** (`/teacher/lessons`) and click **"Create Lesson"**. Use the rich editor to add text, video, and interactive content.

**Q: How do I track student progress?**
A: Use the **Student Roster** (`/teacher/students`) to see individual progress, or visit **Analytics** (`/teacher/analytics`) for class-wide insights.

**Q: Can I reorder lessons in my course?**
A: Yes. In the **Lesson Content** page, drag and drop lessons to change their order.

### Administrators

**Q: How do I update iqamah times?**
A: Go to **Prayer Times** in the admin portal, select the month, and update the times. Changes appear immediately on the public website.

**Q: How do I export analytics?**
A: Visit the **Analytics** page and click the **CSV** or **PDF** export buttons.
